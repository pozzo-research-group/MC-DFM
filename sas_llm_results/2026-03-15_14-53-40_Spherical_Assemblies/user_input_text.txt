Simulate the scattering of a mixture of equal proportions of assemblies of spheres of 10 nm diameter. The assemblies include sphere monomers, sphere doublets, triplets which are spheres assemblied in a triangular shape, tetrahedral which are spheres assembled in a triangular shape with another sphere on the center of the triangle but not overlapping with any other sphere, and trigonal dipyrimidal which are the tetrahedrals with an extra sphere on the other side of the triangle. Do not allow any spheres to overlap with each other.
